Music

I am a true deewana of pop music — it has been a part of my life for the last eight years, and I honestly can’t imagine myself without it. From the moment I plug in my earphones, the world around me fades away and it’s just me and the beats. My playlist is like a treasure chest filled with songs that have shaped my moods and memories — “Brown Rang”, “Blinding Lights”, “On The Floor”, “Ignite”, “Starboy”, “Summer High”, “Intension”, “Headlight”, “Heat Waves”, “Hymn for the Weekend”, “Stereo”, “Harleys in Hawaii”, “Be With You" — and countless Hindi songs that are close to my heart. Whether it’s the energy of an EDM drop, the smooth vibe of pop, or the emotional touch of Hindi melodies, music has been my constant companion, my stress-buster, and my inspiration. For me, music is not just sound — it’s a feeling, a lifestyle, and an inseparable part of who I am.

Gaming

The second thing I have loved the most is gaming—sometimes even more than studying. I still remember those days when an important exam was just around the corner, and instead of revising my incomplete syllabus, I would sit glued to the screen for hours, lost in another world. Over the years, I’ve played countless games, but only a few have truly stayed with me. The one that has captured my heart the most is "HALO". My connection with this series is beyond words—I’ve played a single mission in Halo: Combat Evolved thousands of times, and every time it feels just as thrilling. The bond with Master Chief, the atmosphere, the soundtrack—everything makes it magical. Apart from HALO, I’ve had unforgettable adventures in "GTA San Andreas" and "GTA Vice City", spending hours exploring their worlds. On mobile, games like "Clash of Clans", "Art of War", and "Free Fire" have been my constant companions, each holding its own set of memories. For me, gaming isn’t just a hobby—it’s a part of who I am.

Electronics Projects

When I was in 7th standard, I started making small electronic projects because I loved creating and experimenting with new things. I made working models like a Fire Brigade, Tank, Boring Machine, Gun, and many more. I also repaired and built some electronic devices on my own. Some of my projects included a battery charger using a 200W, 220V-to-24V/12V step-down transformer, a spark welding machine using high current, and an audio amplifier with MOSFETs. I enjoyed working on these projects until the COVID-19 pandemic, when classes shifted online. During that time, I began focusing more on my studies and developed a strong interest in Physics.

Movies

Since childhood, certain movies have shaped my imagination and values in remarkable ways. Science fiction films like Avatar, The Day the Earth Stood Still, Ender’s Game, X-Men, and various Marvel productions — with Iron Man being my favorite — opened my mind to the wonders of technology, innovation, and limitless possibilities. Animated masterpieces such as The Croods, How to Train Your Dragon, Up, The Lion King, Shrek, and Ice Age etc.. nurtured empathy, courage, and resilience within me. Together, these films not only entertained me but also inspired a vision for life, teaching lessons of creativity, responsibility, and the beauty of human potential.
