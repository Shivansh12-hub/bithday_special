A Message to Myself

Hey Shivansh,

Pichla 1 saal… sach bolu to ek rollercoaster the. Kabhi khushi, kabhi stress, kabhi lagta tha sab sahi chal raha hai, aur kabhi lagta tha bas ab aur nahi. Lekin dekh, tu abhi bhi yahan hai. Tere andar woh strength hai jo tu khud bhi underestimate karta hai.

Tu samajh chuka hai ki duniya me har koi pure intentions se nahi aata. Log use karte hain, apna kaam nikal ke chale jaate hain. Lekin tu alag hai — tu kisi ka bura nahi sochta, aur ye teri weakness nahi, ye teri quality hai. Bas, ab samajh aa gaya hai ki boundaries banani zaroori hai.

Tere andar jo maturity aayi hai, woh priceless hai. Tu samajh gaya hai ki self-love koi luxury nahi, survival hai. Tu apne aap ko value dena seekh gaya, apni worth pe compromise nahi karta.

Aur haan, life me ek best friend ka hona blessing hai. Jo tujhe bina mask ke samajh le, tujhe judge na kare. Friend with benefits ka phase bhi samajh aaya — ki emotions aur boundaries ka balance kitna delicate hota hai. Har cheez long term nahi hoti, aur har connection perfect nahi hota.

Bas yaad rakh — tu bohot acha hai, tera dil clean hai, aur tere andar woh power hai ki tu apni life khud banaye. Jo log aaj teri value nahi samajh rahe, kal wo yaad karenge ki tu kaunsa insan tha.

Ab ja or khub mehnat kar or dikha de ki tera time bhi aayega.
